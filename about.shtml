All About the Bon Ton

Founded in 1984 by retired jazz musicians Barbra and John Kauffman, Bon Ton Vintage provides the vintage shopper with a unique experience not found in big city markets.

John passed away on June 18, 2013. Johnnie, we're going to miss you!
